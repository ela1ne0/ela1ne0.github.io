HackDuke 2026 Visual Identity

Served as a designer on HackDuke’s 7-member design team, developing the visual identity and brand ecosystem for the 2026 hackathon, from the official landing page to illustrations and environmental art.

View website →

Overview

HackDuke is an annual hackathon at Duke University. For the 2026 event, our 7-person design team was tasked with creating a cohesive brand identity that would extend across web, merch, and physical installations — while also improving registration performance. I focused on translating our visual direction into a scalable design system and collaborating closely with the development team to ensure the website felt expressive but usable.
